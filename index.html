About Me

I’m Kamran Khan, a Writer, Animation Director, and Teacher with nearly two decades in the animation industry. I’ve worked on iconic projects like 3 Bahadur (Pakistan’s first 3D animated feature film) and Netflix’s Sitara: Let Girls Dream as Head of Production and Animation Director at Waadi Animations. In 2020, I founded Final Act Animations, a Karachi-based studio specializing in 3D character animation. We’ve worked on projects like Team Muhafiz, expanding into 3D asset development and storyboarding. Beyond production, I’ve been teaching animation since 2006, mentoring students at top institutes in Pakistan. Passionate about storytelling and pushing creative boundaries, I continue to shape the future of animation—one frame at a time!
